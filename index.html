Chameleon Scarf

Using a wearable arduino, LEDs, conductive thread, and a sensor I created a scarf that could change colors based on what someone's wearing. I sewed the LEDs spaced out along a ribbon connected with conductive thread with the arduino and sensor at one end. I programmed the arduino to signal the sensor to flash three times before registering the color of what was on top of it. By doing this users are able to place a portion of what they're wearing on top of the sensor which then changes all of the LEDs to be that color. I then covered this base with cheese cloth to diffuse the light dispersion better and then encompassed it in a knit white scarf. Fluffy white scarf by day, Electric color coordinated accessory by night.
